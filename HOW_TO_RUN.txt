IMPORTANT FIXES
- The complete original account/policy schema is retained.
- Null and empty-string keys are retained.
- Only policy.line.risk is generated dynamically.
- Member count controls the exact number of risk objects.
- The first risk member matches the account name, DOB, gender and salutation.
- FamilyType is generated as 1A, 2A, 2A+1C, etc.
- All 15 coverage objects remain present.
- Selected coverage gets Indicator "1"; unselected coverage gets "0".
- SequenceID is created automatically.

DATE FIELDS
- Proposal Date and Effective Date are required on the webpage.
- Their entered YYYY-MM-DD values are written directly into policy.ProposalDate and policy.EffectiveDate.
- Placeholder values such as {{ProposalDate}} and {{EffectiveDate}} are no longer generated.
